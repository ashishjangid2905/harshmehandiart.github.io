About Me

Harsh Sharma

Coming from Pilani, Rajasthan is practicing this art for the last 25+ years and have always been committed to providing artistic, professional services and making my clients experience with henna/mehndi a unique, enjoyable and an unforgettable one.

Since childhood, when i was pursuing my career with my proffesional degree, i always felt that there is sometihing missing in my life for which i am pasonate of, hence after so many hurdles i decided to follow my passion and since very young age i started practising mehndi and slowely slowely i saw people are so happy with my work. I got my path and career too and since then i am actively serving and now it is more than 25 years. I enjoy my work and try my 100 % to fulfil the clients desire in the form of art for thier special occassion.
